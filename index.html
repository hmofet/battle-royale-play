BATTLE ROYALE

A run-and-gun pixel deathmatch — couch co-op and online.

▶ Play in Browser ⬇ Download for Windows

Browser: no install, loads ~36 MB. Windows: ~35 MB zip (unzip & run BattleRoyale.exe). On macOS / Linux? Use Play in Browser — no install needed.

🔒 Online is invite-only beta. Choose Online (Beta) → Railway, type the passphrase you were given, then Connect. (No passphrase = no entry.)
Then Create Room (share the 4-letter code) or Quickmatch / Join by Code; ↑/↓ choose your input (keyboard or gamepad), add a CPU or wait for a friend, Space ready, Enter start. Need 2+ players.

Controls (Player 1)

  • A/D move, W/S aim up/down
  • Space jump · F shoot
  • Q melee · E special / call-in
  • Gamepads supported (couch co-op up to 8)

What is this?

Pick from a roster of action-movie bros, each with a unique weapon and a devastating call-in. Destructible terrain, exploding everything, free-for-all to the kill target. All art and sound generated in code.

Windows may warn “unknown publisher” (the build is unsigned) — click More info → Run anyway.
Made with Godot. Online play runs on a dedicated server. · Beta build.
